# Rhode Rash Design System & Brand Guidelines

Rhode Rash is a bold, energetic media brand focused on politics, culture, and innovation in the Ocean State. This document outlines the core visual components and design principles that define the Rhode Rash brand.

## 1. Brand Essence
- **Personality:** Bold, irreverent, authoritative yet accessible, fast-paced, and modern.
- **Tone:** Sharp analysis with a local Rhode Island flavor.
- **Tagline:** Bold takes on the Ocean State.

## 2. Visual Identity (Logos)
The logo features a stylized, high-contrast illustration of the Rhode Island State House, combined with aggressive, sharp-edged typography.

- **Main Logo:** A multi-layered emblem with a blue-to-pink gradient.
- **Symbolism:** The dome represents the seat of power (politics), while the dynamic "RASH" lettering conveys impact and innovation.

## 3. Color Palette
The palette is a vibrant, high-energy combination of deep blues and hot pinks, creating a signature "Electric Ocean" gradient.

| Color | Hex | RGB | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Blue** | `#1e30f3` | `30, 48, 243` | Primary buttons, headers, brand accents. |
| **Primary Pink** | `#e21e80` | `226, 30, 128` | Highlights, accents, secondary brand color. |
| **Deep Dark** | `#212529` | `33, 37, 41` | Body text, dark mode backgrounds. |
| **Clean White** | `#ffffff` | `255, 255, 255` | Backgrounds, high-contrast text on dark colors. |

**Gradient:**
`linear-gradient(135deg, #1e30f3 0%, #e21e80 100%)` (Used for "text-gradient" and "bg-gradient-primary-to-secondary")

## 4. Typography
Rhode Rash uses clean, modern sans-serif typefaces to balance the complexity of the logo.

- **Primary Font:** **Plus Jakarta Sans** (Sans-serif)
    - Used for body text, UI elements, and subheadings.
    - Standard Weight: 400
    - Bold Weight: 700
- **Monospace Font:** SFMono-Regular, Consolas, or Courier New.
    - Used for technical data or specialized snippets.

## 5. UI & Design Principles
- **Rounded Corners:** A standard `border-radius: 1rem` (rounded-4) is used for cards and hero images to soften the sharp edges of the logo.
- **High Contrast:** Text should always maintain high legibility, using white on dark gradients or the primary blue on white backgrounds.
- **Dynamic Elements:** Use of dot patterns (dots-1 through dots-4) for decorative texture and background depth.
- **Action Elements:**
    - Primary Buttons: Solid Blue (`#1e30f3`) with white text.
    - Secondary Buttons: Outlined with the primary gradient or solid white on dark backgrounds.

## 6. Layout Strategy
- **Hero Sections:** Large-scale logos paired with bold, gradient-filled headlines.
- **Content Cards:** Clean white backgrounds with subtle shadows to lift content off the page.
- **Emphasis:** Critical labels (Politics, Culture, Innovation) use a small, uppercase badge style with gradient backgrounds.
